Audio Analysis Accuracy: Manual vs Program

This page documents two manual test comparisons between professional audio tools (Voxengo Span, Waves PAZ, Waves InPhase) and the automated analysis metrics generated by the ZoundZcope backend. The goal is to transparently demonstrate how closely the program's audio analysis matches manual measurements and expert listening impressions.

Note: This analysis focuses on the loudest sections of the track rather than the entire timeline. Metrics like LUFS, RMS peak, and dynamic range are calculated from these high-energy moments to better reflect perceived loudness and match professional metering tools. This may lead to more aligned results with manual inspection tools (e.g., Youlean, SPAN), though slight discrepancies can still arise due to plugin-specific methods and smoothing algorithms.
